Lucas Pesenti

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Statistics and Computer Science at Bocconi University. Before that, I studied at École Normale Supérieure de Paris and got my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the Parisian Master of Research in Computer Science.

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, with a preference for approximation algorithms, average-case analysis, and the role of randomness in computation. My current research projects consist in leveraging techniques from online optimization to tackle discrepancy and sparsification problems.
